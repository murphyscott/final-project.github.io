Democratic Candidate: Joe Biden

History

President Elect Biden is the democratic party representative for the 2020 election. After graduating from the University of Delaware in 1965 with a degree in political science and history he received his law degree from Syracuse University in 1968. Joe Biden served as a trial attorney, the senator for Delaware, and as the Vice President to President Barack Obama. He became one of the youngest senators at age 29 and also one of the longest serving senators (serving for 36 years).
