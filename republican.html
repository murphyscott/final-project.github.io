Republican Candidate

History

President Trump is the republican candidate for the 2020 election. He served as the 45th President of the United States alongside his vice president Mike Pence. After graduating from Wharton Business School with a degree in Economics in 1968 he found himself in the real estate business. In addition to creating his own company named The Trump Organization in 1971, he also has written a book, appeared on talk shows, and had a popular TV series called The Apprentice.
